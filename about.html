In Her Own Write is a Scorpio ENFP 4w3. Viewers are highly recommended to read the Origin Story here.

Bored in quarantine, I had the insatiable urge to make a blog and talk about 'stuff'. With the help of my little coding superpowers, In Her Own Write was founded on April 3 2020, but the website was officially published on April 5. There was also a podcast of the same name in 2021, but I kind of forgot about it lol. Here is the link to the show; it is in Cantonese and it is quite embarrassing, so I highly encourage NOT to check it out. Maybe I will restart the show when I'm employed and have more time.

Fast forward to 2025, I am now graduating from UC Berkeley with an Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences degree. I am slowly morphing into the very thing I swore to destroy (a Silicon Valley techbro); so to reignite my passion for life, I want to pick up the habit of writing again and utilize this platform 2020 me built for myself. No Substack, just ambition and code.

The main reason for me starting this is to show some other sides of me as a person. I have always suspected people don't take me too seriously as a person due to my silly, unserious demeanor in person. For a more detailed breakdown (pun-intended), please read this post here. We all want to show the best sides of ourselves, and this is the serious, emotional side of me. I will try to comfortably open up and talk about stuff. Not like super personal stuff though, just other parts of my brain that deserves everyone's attention. I hope this won't be awkward cuz really, this is my place to talk and I get to do my own show.

I've always imagined myself as a stand-up comedian, talking about my anecdotes and unnecessary opinions (that no one asked). My time to shine. Hope I won't get cancelled for this. Some sections are password-protected for that very reason.
